# Very Good AI - Brand Identity Guidelines

This document outlines the core brand identity for **Very Good AI**, derived from the official logo. It is designed to be used as a reference for landing pages, web applications, and marketing materials to ensure a consistent, clean, and trustworthy user experience.

## 1. Core Brand Personality
*   **Trustworthy:** Professional, reliable, and secure.
*   **Modern & Clean:** Uncluttered, straightforward, and cutting-edge.
*   **Forward-Thinking:** Represented by the upward arrow—focused on growth, efficiency, and progress.

---

## 2. Color Palette

The color palette is built around the contrast between deep, stable dark tones and the vibrant, energetic blue from the logo's arrow.

### Primary Colors
*   **Tech Cyan (The Arrow): `#00BFFF` (or `#05B2FA`)**
    *   *Usage:* Primary call-to-action (CTA) buttons, links, important highlights, and icons. This is the brand's signature color.
*   **Deep Charcoal (The Text): `#1A1E24`**
    *   *Usage:* Primary text color, headings, and footer backgrounds. It provides a softer, more modern contrast than pure black, increasing readability while maintaining authority.

### Secondary & Accent Colors
*   **Electric Blue (Gradient Accent): `#007BFF`**
    *   *Usage:* Use alongside Tech Cyan to create subtle gradients (mimicking the swoop on the 'A') or for hover states on primary buttons.
*   **Clean White: `#FFFFFF`**
    *   *Usage:* The primary background color. Ample white space is crucial to achieving the "clean and clear" aesthetic.
*   **Light Slate Gray: `#F4F6F9`**
    *   *Usage:* Backgrounds for secondary sections, cards, or subtle borders to separate content areas without cluttering the page.

---

## 3. Typography

The logo features a condensed, geometric sans-serif font for "VERY GOOD" and a bold, stable sans-serif for "AI". The web fonts should mirror this modern, highly legible aesthetic.

### Primary Font (Headings): **Inter** or **Montserrat**
*   *Why:* Geometric, modern, and highly legible. It feels tech-forward but very stable.
*   *Weights:* Bold (700) or Extra Bold (800) for large headers to match the weight of the "AI" in the logo.

### Secondary Font (Body Text): **Roboto** or **Open Sans**
*   *Why:* Extremely clean, optimized for screen reading, and neutral enough not to distract from the brand colors.
*   *Weights:* Regular (400) for standard text, Medium (500) for sub-text or secondary buttons.

---

## 4. Design Principles for the Landing Page

To translate this identity into a landing page, follow these UI/UX guidelines:

1.  **Embrace Whitespace:** Do not crowd elements. Let the text and the bright cyan accents breathe. This builds trust and looks premium.
2.  **Subtle Gradients:** The arrow in the logo has a dynamic sweep. Incorporate subtle cyan-to-blue gradients in buttons or background graphic accents, but keep them minimal so they don't overpower the page.
3.  **Sharp but Friendly:** Use slight border-radiuses on buttons and cards (e.g., `4px` or `8px`)—not completely sharp, but not overly pill-shaped. This strikes a balance between a strict tech company and an accessible tool.
4.  **High Contrast:** Always ensure the dark charcoal text pops against the white or light gray backgrounds for maximum accessibility and a crisp look.
